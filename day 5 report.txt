A Comparison between Selenium and QTP


Quick Test Professional(QTP) is a proprietary automated testing tool previously owned by the company Mercury Interactive before it was acquired by Hewlett-Packard in 2006

Advantages and Benefits of Selenium over QTP
• Selenium Open source, free to use, and free of charge.
•QTP is Commercial.

Advantages of QTP over Selenium
•QTP Can test both web and desktop applications
•Selenium can only test web applications

QTP has more advanced capabilities, Selenium outweighs QTP in three main areas:
•cost
•flexibility
•Parallel testing
